Bio

You may be wondering, who made this website after all? Yes, it was Viktor Mellow himself. So why does he insists on refering to himself on the third person all over it? Because it seems more professional, of course! Hope you don't mind this fourth wall joke, however, there's actually a point behind it. My work truly is handcrafted and independent, I really do it out of love for music, arts and rock n' roll. Constantly I ask myself if it's worth it and, thought I can't really say it is, I just keep on doing it.

Viktor Mellow, or oficially Vitor Araujo Mellado, was born in São Paulo, Brazil in 1992. Influenced by friends who wanted to form a band he started having classical guitar lessons at 14 and, though he and these friends never got to start a band, he switched to the electric guitar at 17. His first real band experiences came a while after that and from them on he passed through several different groups, ranging from heavy metal, classic rock to pop and even comedy music. Unsatisfied with these band experiences he decided to work on solo efforts with the help of a few dear music collaborators and friends.

Education

Viktor Mellow is actually a bachellor in classical music composition and his Alma Mater is UNESP, one of the most respected institutions in Brazil. This course had an emphasis on electroacoustic music wich allowed him to expand his music training to audio production as well. He also took a technical course in choral conducting.

Teaching

Viktor started teaching music in 2014, focusing mostly on guitar and theory, though he taught a few bass guitar lessons too. During college he also took part in an academic program called Tutorial Education Program in wich he got involved with a number of academic researches and projects.

Whether you're music fan who wants to play your favorite tunes, or are an experienced player who wants to take your technique and musical understanding to new heights we can arrange a free demonstrative class for you.
